ISDIRECTORY (tiedosto)

  • tiedosto = Merkkijono, tiedostonimi.

    • Katso myös: FILEEXISTS

       ESIMERKKI
    AddText "Input a filename (try also folders)"

    Repeat
        k$=Input("::")
        DrawScreen
    Until KeyHit(cbkeyreturn)

    CloseInput
    ClearText

    isfile=FileExists(k$)

    Text 10,30,"FILE EXISTS: "+isfile
    Text 10,50,"IS FOLDER? "+IsDirectory(k$)
    DrawScreen

    WaitKey
